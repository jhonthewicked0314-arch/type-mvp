Ergonomics & Posture

Typing fast isn't just about your fingers; it's about your whole body. Prevent strain and increase endurance with proper form.

  • Keep your wrists hovering, not resting on the desk.
  • Position your monitor at eye level to prevent neck strain.
  • Keep your feet flat on the floor for core stability.
